Market perspective

  • Selective opportunity: dispersion across sectors and issuers increasingly rewards portfolios guided by disciplined conviction rather than broad market exposure alone.
  • Liquidity awareness: portfolio resilience depends on balancing long-term ideas with the realities of market depth and execution flexibility.
  • Stewardship matters: clients value managers who can articulate not only what they own, but why positions belong in the portfolio and how they are governed.
